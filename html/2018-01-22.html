@nimanthaharshana it’s fairly easy
build two sites into “subdirectories"
so yourdomain.com/site1/ and yourdomain.com/site2/
then use the .htaccess or nginx rewrite depending on your setup to direct the domain to site1 or site2
the rest of the directory / master structures are up to you. Usually in cases like this we might have 2 or more master files.
