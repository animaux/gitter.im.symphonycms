At the moment there is some sort of turbulence here in Germany because of EU-GDPR. But I don't have any "solutions" yet. Regarding cookies: Symphony does not set cookies unless you try to login to your backend or Members account (or have special extensions/events running which write to the user session, e.g. the Storage extension). Re: personal data — yes, there will be some additional pressure to follow "best practices", e.g. encryption of backups, as mentioned by @jonmifsud. However, I don't really understand why @jonmifsud promotes separation of app and database server in this context.
